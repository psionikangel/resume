I am a software engineer with 10 years experience that specializes in DevOps roles. Technically speaking, I see myself as a generalist, I learn quickly and always had an interest in learning new technologies. This led me to learn a lot of usefull skills over the years, most often by going out of my comfort zone to tackle a particularly challenging technical problem. I am a challenge-driven person and technology enthusiast that is well at ease in both technology oriented and people oriented roles. I prefer to work within a team sharing a common objective, most likely solving problems through technology and innovation.

I strongly believe that a lean software development process backed up by the best tools is key to innovation and success. This is why this resume is hosted in a Nginx Docker container hosted on Amazon EC2 with automated SSL certificates from Let's Encrypt. Every management task for this site is automated with Ansible. Also, you can check the source code on Github. It might be overkill for a simple resume, but it shows that my experience is not just a list of buzzwords. Plus, it was really fun to do something out of the ordinary.
